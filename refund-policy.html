Refund & Return Policy

For demonstration purposes, AUREN models a customer-friendly 7-day return window for unused products, size exchanges subject to stock availability, and refund initiation after item inspection. Orders marked as delivered may show UI actions for return or exchange requests, while recent orders may surface cancellation controls before they progress too far in fulfilment. Promo discounts are proportionally adjusted on refunded items. In a production implementation, return logistics, reverse pickup SLAs, non-returnable categories, wallet refunds, and damage claim procedures should be connected to actual operations and payment systems.

Use of the demo

This storefront exists as a polished front-end showcase and learning project. Any production deployment should add real authentication, secure checkout processing, backend order validation, rate limiting, observability, and legal review before use with actual customers.

Operational note

All product images are placeholder assets served from Picsum. Brand stories, customer records, order IDs, metrics, and policy copy are synthetic and intended only to make the interface feel complete and realistic.
